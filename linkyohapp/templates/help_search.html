Search Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Linkyoh search guide! Finding the right service provider is easy with our powerful search tools. Whether you're looking for a specific service or just browsing what's available in your area, this guide will help you navigate our search features effectively.

Linkyoh offers multiple ways to find services:

  • Quick search using the search bar
  • Advanced search with filters
  • Browsing by categories
  • Location-based searching

This guide will walk you through each method to help you find exactly what you're looking for.

Quick Search Guide

If you're looking to find services quickly, here's how to get started:

Find Services in 3 Simple Steps
  1. Use the search bar at the top of any page
  2. Enter keywords related to the service you need (e.g., "plumber", "electrician", "tutor")
  3. Press Enter or click the search icon to see results

For more specific results, continue reading to learn about our advanced search features and filters.

Using the Search Bar

The search bar is available at the top of every page on Linkyoh, making it easy to search from anywhere on the site.

1Effective Search Terms

Our search engine looks for matches in multiple fields, including:

  • Service titles
  • Service descriptions
  • Categories and subcategories
  • Locations and districts
Search For Example Search Terms What It Will Find
Service Type "plumber", "electrician", "tutor" Services with these words in their title or description
Specific Need "leaky faucet", "math tutor", "house painting" Services that mention these specific needs
Location "Belize City", "San Pedro", "Orange Walk" Services available in these locations
Pro Tip

Use specific terms for better results. For example, instead of searching for "home repair," try "kitchen cabinet installation" or "bathroom tile repair" to find more specialized service providers.

Advanced Search

For more precise results, use our advanced search page which offers additional filtering options.

1Accessing Advanced Search

You can access the advanced search page in two ways:

  • Click on the search icon in the navigation bar
  • Enter a search term and then refine your results using the filters on the search results page
2Advanced Search Features

The advanced search page offers several powerful features:

Multiple Filters

Combine different filters to narrow down results exactly as needed

Location-Based Search

Find services available in specific districts or locations

Category Filtering

Browse services by main categories and subcategories

Price Range

Set minimum and maximum price ranges for services

Using Filters

Filters help you narrow down search results to find exactly what you're looking for. Here's how to use them effectively:

Filter Description How to Use
Category Filter by main service category Select from the dropdown menu to see services in that category only
Subcategory Filter by more specific service type First select a category, then choose from available subcategories
District Filter by district in Belize Select a district to see services available in that area
Location Filter by specific location within a district First select a district, then choose from available locations
Price Range Filter by minimum and maximum price Enter minimum and/or maximum price values to see services within that range
Pro Tip

You can combine multiple filters for more precise results. For example, you can search for "plumbers" in "Belize City" with prices between "$50" and "$100".

1Applying Filters
  1. Enter your search term in the search bar (optional)
  2. Select filters from the available options
  3. The results will update automatically as you apply filters
  4. To remove a filter, click the "Clear" button next to it or select a different option

Browsing Categories

If you're not sure exactly what you're looking for, browsing by category can help you discover available services.

1Using the Category Navigation

The category navigation bar is located just below the main navigation on every page:

  1. Click on any category in the navigation bar to see all services in that category
  2. On the category page, you can further filter by subcategory, location, and price
  3. You can also sort results by newest, oldest, price (low to high), or price (high to low)
2Popular Categories

On the home page, you'll find a section showcasing popular categories:

  1. Click on any category card to see all services in that category
  2. These categories are determined by the number of available services and user activity
Pro Tip

Browsing categories is a great way to discover services you might not have thought to search for directly. Take some time to explore different categories to see what's available in your area.

Search Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of Linkyoh's search features:

Use Specific Keywords

The more specific your search terms, the more relevant your results will be. For example, "kitchen plumber" instead of just "plumber".

Start with Location

If proximity is important, start by filtering for your district or location before applying other filters.

Use Sorting Options

After searching, use the sort options to arrange results by newest, price, or other criteria that matter most to you.

Broaden Your Search

If you're not finding what you need, try using more general terms or fewer filters to see more options.

Save Favorites

When you find services you like, click the heart icon to save them to your favorites for easy access later.

Check Recently Viewed

Your recently viewed services are saved automatically, making it easy to return to listings you've already seen.

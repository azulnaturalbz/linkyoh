How we do it

Linkyoh relies on its member's experience, reviews and likes that aren’t submitted anonymously. Linkyoh members’ information is always kept secure and confidential on Linkyoh. Reviews are made available to companies and persons who have been reviews. Since there are always two sides to every story, companies can respond to reviews, helping to make sure that members get all the information they need to make a hiring or contracting decision. Additionally, members can only report Linkyoh quality of service once every six months, ensuring no one can favor one service provider over another.

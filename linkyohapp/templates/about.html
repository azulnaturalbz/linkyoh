How Linkyoh Works

1

Collecting Experiences

From automotive specialists to health professionals, we gather authentic experiences from users. With the power of numbers, we help determine the best service providers through organic feedback that helps future clients find perfect matches.

2

Transparent Reviews

Linkyoh relies on member experiences, reviews, and likes that aren't submitted anonymously. Member information is kept secure and confidential. Reviews are made available to companies who have been reviewed, and they can respond to ensure both sides of every story are heard.

3

Quality Control

All reviews submitted by members go through our team of Linkyoh collaborators who look for discrepancies or red flags. Companies caught reporting on their own services are reprimanded, and those reviews are promptly removed. Members can only report on service quality once every six months to prevent favoritism.

Gig Creation Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Linkyoh gig creation guide! A "gig" is a service listing that showcases what you offer to potential customers. Creating a well-crafted gig is essential to attract customers and grow your business on our platform.

This guide will walk you through the entire process of creating a gig, explaining each field and section of the form. Whether you're looking to create a simple listing quickly or a detailed service offering with multiple images and contact options, we've got you covered.

Quick Start Guide

If you're in a hurry and want to create a simple gig quickly, follow these steps:

Create a Simple Gig in 5 Minutes
  1. Go to the Create Gig page
  2. Fill in the required fields in the Basic Info tab:
    • Gig Title
    • Category & Subcategory
    • Description
    • Price (or check "Call for pricing")
    • Main Phone Number
    • District & Location
    • Address
    • Main Photo
  3. Skip the other tabs (Images, Contact Info, Service Areas) - these are optional
  4. Click "Publish Gig" at the bottom of the form
  5. That's it! Your gig is now live on Linkyoh

For a more detailed listing that stands out from the competition, continue reading this guide to learn about all the available options.

Basic Information

The Basic Information tab contains the essential details about your service. All fields in this section are required unless noted as optional.

1Basic Information Fields
Field Description Tips
Gig Title Required The name of your service (max 500 characters) Be clear and specific. Include keywords that potential customers might search for.
Category Required The main category your service belongs to Choose the most relevant category for your service to help customers find you.
Subcategory Required A more specific classification within the main category The subcategory options will change based on the category you select.
Description Required Detailed information about your service (max 1000 characters) Include what you offer, your experience, and why customers should choose you.
2Pricing Information
Field Description Tips
Call for pricing Optional Check this if you prefer customers to call you for pricing details Useful for services where pricing varies based on customer needs.
Price Required The cost of your service in dollars If you checked "Call for pricing," this field will be hidden and set to a special value.
3Contact Information
Field Description Tips
Main Phone Required Your primary contact number in international format Use the format +5016550000 for Belize numbers.
4Location Information
Field Description Tips
District Required The district where your service is primarily offered Select the district where you're based or primarily operate.
Location Required The specific location within the district The location options will change based on the district you select.
Address 1 Required Your primary address Enter your street address, house number, etc.
Address 2 Optional Additional address information Use this for apartment numbers, building names, etc.
5Media
Field Description Tips
Main Photo Required The primary image for your gig Use a high-quality image that clearly represents your service. Supported formats: jpg, jpeg, png, gif.
6Status
Field Description Tips
Gig Status Required Whether your gig is active (visible) or disabled (hidden) Set to "Active" to make your gig visible to customers immediately. Set to "Disabled" if you're not ready to publish yet.
Pro Tip

For a simple gig, you only need to fill out the Basic Info tab. The other tabs (Images, Contact Info, Service Areas) are optional and can be added later to enhance your listing.

Images (Optional)

The Images tab allows you to add up to 10 additional images to showcase your service from different angles or highlight specific features.

Field Description Tips
Image File Optional Additional image for your gig Use high-quality images that showcase different aspects of your service.
Caption Optional A description for the image Use captions to explain what the image shows or highlight specific features.
Set as primary image Optional Makes this image the main image for your gig Check this to replace the main photo with this image.
Display Order Optional The order in which images appear Lower numbers appear first. Leave blank to use the default order.
Pro Tip

High-quality images can significantly increase customer interest. Include images that show your work process, before/after results, or your team in action.

Contact Information (Optional)

The Contact Info tab allows you to add up to 5 additional contact numbers to make it easier for customers to reach you through different channels.

Field Description Tips
Phone Number Optional Additional contact number in international format Use the format +5016550000 for Belize numbers.
Description Optional A label for this contact number Examples: "Business", "Mobile", "Office", "WhatsApp only"
Available on WhatsApp Optional Indicates if this number can be contacted via WhatsApp Check this if customers can reach you on WhatsApp using this number.
Set as primary contact Optional Makes this the primary contact number for your gig Check this to replace the main phone number with this one.
Display Order Optional The order in which contact numbers appear Lower numbers appear first. Leave blank to use the default order.
Pro Tip

Providing multiple contact options increases the chances of customers reaching you. Consider adding a dedicated business line and a WhatsApp number for messaging.

Service Areas (Optional)

The Service Areas tab allows you to add up to 5 additional areas where you offer your services to reach more customers in different locations.

Field Description Tips
District Optional Additional district where you offer your service Select districts where you're willing to travel or provide services.
Location Optional Specific location within the additional district The location options will change based on the district you select.
Description Optional Additional information about this service area Examples: "Weekends only", "Extra fee applies", "By appointment only"
Set as primary area Optional Makes this the primary service area for your gig Check this to replace the main location with this one.
Display Order Optional The order in which service areas appear Lower numbers appear first. Leave blank to use the default order.
Pro Tip

Adding multiple service areas can significantly increase your visibility in search results. If you're willing to travel to provide your service, be sure to add those areas here.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help your gig stand out and attract more customers:

Write a Clear Title

Use specific keywords that potential customers might search for. Be clear about what you offer.

Use High-Quality Images

Clear, well-lit photos of your work will attract more customers. Show before/after results if applicable.

Detailed Description

Explain what sets your service apart, your experience, and why customers should choose you.

Multiple Contact Options

Make it easy for customers to reach you by providing multiple contact methods.

Add Multiple Service Areas

Increase your visibility by adding all the areas where you're willing to provide your service.

Clear Pricing

Be transparent about your pricing or check "Call for pricing" if your rates vary based on the job.
